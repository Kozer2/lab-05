About Me

Hello, my name is Ben Hemann and I am a programmer. I am a graduate of the University of Iowa where I took social informatics. That is a degree that combines computer science with sociology. Before that I was in the Marine Corps for 6 years as an active duty Marine. I spent 3 of those years overseas working at US Embassies. Currently I am a student at the DeltaV code school in Cedar Rapids where I am learning intensive programming skills. My time in the Marines showed me that no matter how hard things get, you can always push on as a team. My time at Iowa showed me that programming is not something I should be afraid of and that working hard, even on something that is completely new to you, is rewarding. And my time at DeltaV has showed me that deadlines are not the end of the world. In my short time programming I have gained skills to build file searching programs using python, websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and geo-location apps using AWS, SQL, HTML, and python.
